Simple Resonator

As a quick introduction to LaserCanvas, we create a simple resonator to illustrate the following points:

  • Dragging optics on the Canvas
  • Inserting lenses and Mirrors
  • Examining properties from the Property Manager

The tutorial is divided into

  • Part 1. Construct the Cavity
  • Part 2. Examine Some Properties
  • Part 3. Add a Lens
  • Part 4. Add a Fold Mirror
