Part 4. Optimize with the Solver

< Prev | Next >

We wish to find an optic spacing that creates a beam size of 2 mm at the collimating lens. As an approximately collimated condition, we specify that we wish the waist position to be 100 m after the lens. In addition, we add Solver penalties for variables x and y should they become negative or larger than 500 mm. See also the Solver section.

  • Create a system Solver by selecting menu Tools | Solver or clicking the  Solver button on the toolbar.
  • In the edit field, enter the expression below to be minimized. The text can wrap over several lines, but must form a valid expression.
(L4.w_sag-2000)^2 + 1e-6*(L4.z0_sag-100000)^2
+ (y<0)*y^2 + (y>500)*(y-500)^2
+ (x<0)*x^2 + (x>500)*(x-500)^2
  • Click the Solve button. For best results, you may wish to click the Solve button several times.
