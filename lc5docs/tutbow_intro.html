Bow-Tie Resonator

In this tutorial, we construct a typical bow-tie resonator with a Brewster-angled crystal. The incidence angle on the curved optics is adjusted to compensate for the astigmatism introduced by the laser crystal.

This tutorial will illustrate

  • Standing-wave resonators
  • Intra-cavity curved mirrors
  • Brewster-angled plate
  • Variables and graphs

The tutorial is divided into

  • Part 1. Construct the Cavity
  • Part 2. Examine the Stability
  • Part 3. Examine Astigmatism
  • Part 4. Optimize Astigmatism
