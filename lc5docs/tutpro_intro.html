Collimating Telescope

We wish to construct a telescope that collimates an initially diverging HeNe laser source to a specific mode size, with the resulting waist at a large distance from the second lens. We wish to find the optimal spacing between two lenses of given focal lengths.

This tutorial will illustrate:

  • Propagation System and its input parameters
  • Lenses, positive and negative
  • Variables
  • Optic Graphs
  • Solver

The tutorial is divided into

  • Part 1. Construct the Cavity
  • Part 2. Apply Variables
  • Part 3. Create Graphs
  • Part 4. Optimize with the Solver
  • Conclusions
